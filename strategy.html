07
Customer waves
Nebius serves three waves of customers. In APJ, Wave 2 is where the growth comes from — vertical AI and enterprise buying a managed platform, not bare-metal GPUs.
Wave 1 · IaaS

Building AI models

Raw GPU compute for training and fine-tuning. Bare metal, latest NVIDIA, lowest cost per FLOP.

Product: AI Cloud

Preferred Networks · Sony AI · Samsung AI · Naver · Sarvam AI

Cycle: POC-driven, weeks–months

Wave 2 · PaaS

Vertical AI & enterprise

Managed inference platform. Buying endpoints, APIs, SLAs — not GPUs. This is where APJ growth comes from.

Product: Token Factory

DBS · Shopee · Coupang · Bandai Namco · Singtel · Paytm

Cycle: Self-serve trial → enterprise contract

Wave 3 · Full stack

Enterprise AI builders

Fine-tuning on proprietary data, governed deployment, avoiding hyperscaler lock-in. Highest-value customer.

Product: AI Cloud + Token Factory

Toyota · AIA · GovTech SG · Accenture APJ · NTT Data

Cycle: Months–quarters, SI-influenced

How we operate

Ride the momentum.

684% growth. ATH stock. Every conversation started this year carries weight that won't exist once the market normalizes. Speed is the strategy.

NVIDIA is the force multiplier.

$2B investment + Reference Platform. Co-present at every event, co-sell into their base. Primary credibility channel.

Sales-embedded from day one.

Every pipeline call. Co-designed campaigns. The hospitality program is jointly owned with AEs and SDRs.

Localize and build.

Adapt central positioning for local markets. Build from scratch where central can't: sovereign AI, Japan trust-building, Indian pricing.
