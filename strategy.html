02
Operating principles

Ride the momentum. Move now.

684% growth. ATH stock. Every conversation started this year carries weight that won't exist once the market normalizes. Speed is the strategy.

Content arsenal first, then distribute.

Localized positioning, segment messaging, whitepapers, talk tracks, decks — built for APJ. Then push through every channel simultaneously.

NVIDIA is the force multiplier.

$2B investment + Reference Platform. Co-present at every event, co-author content, co-sell into their base. Primary credibility channel.

Sales-embedded, not adjacent.

Every pipeline call. Co-designed campaigns. Marketing puts sales in the room and arms them with content that closes.

Phase 1Positioning & messaging

Localized positioning by ICP

  • APJ value prop per segment: AI-native, enterprise, public sector
  • Vertical messaging: fintech, media, healthcare, government
  • Competitive positioning vs. hyperscalers + local clouds
  • Sovereign AI: European-HQ, non-hyperscaler alternative
  • Market-specific: JP (trust), KR (tech), IN (cost/scale), SG (innovation)

Earnings momentum narrative

  • "Why Nebius, why now" — 684%, $3.4B guidance, 45% margins, $9.3B cash
  • Momentum proof deck: pipeline 3.5× QoQ, Meta $12B, NVIDIA $2B
  • "The neocloud that's actually scaling" — vs. competitors on trajectory
  • Investor-grade credibility for procurement vendor research
Phase 2Content arsenal

Case studies & customer evidence

  • 8+ APJ case studies in 12 months — SG first, then JP/KR
  • Vertical proof points: fintech cost, media deployment, sovereign AI
  • Video testimonials for events and social
  • Reference customer program — logos for panels and calls

Whitepapers, talk tracks & decks

  • Flagship: "The neocloud advantage for APJ" — TCO, architecture, sovereignty
  • Vertical papers: ASEAN fintech, JP media, IN startups
  • Talk track library: 5-min / 20-min / 45-min per segment
  • Event decks, podcast prep, booth scripts — EN, JP, KR
Phase 3Aggressive distribution

Every event. Every podcast. Every stage.

  • Tier-1 sponsorship: GITEX Asia, SG FinTech Fest, Japan IT Week, Cloud Expo, KubeCon
  • NVIDIA: GTC APJ, AI Summit, Inception events
  • Podcast tour: Tech in Asia, The Ken, JP/IN/AI shows
  • Conference speaking: every CFP, 5+ speaker roster
  • Developer meetups: monthly SG → Tokyo, Bangalore, Seoul
  • Industry bodies: AISG, JDLA, nasscom, KAIA

Executive outreach to target industries

  • Target 100+ CTOs/CIOs: fintech, media, healthcare, govt, AI startups
  • Personalized outreach: earnings story + segment positioning
  • CTO roundtables (8-12), private briefings, analyst-hosted events
  • Gartner/IDC/Forrester APJ co-hosted roundtables
  • All conversations tracked in Salesforce as pipeline inputs
